Zero Defect Policy

At Rudra Technocast, quality is not just a department—it's an integral part of our manufacturing culture. We employ a comprehensive Quality Management System to monitor, control, and improve every aspect of our foundry operations.

Our fully equipped laboratory and skilled quality control engineers ensure that every casting meets the exact metallurgical and dimensional specifications required by our clients before dispatch.

Advanced Lab

Testing Capabilities

Our in-house laboratory is equipped with calibrated, state-of-the-art testing equipment.

Spectrometer

Optical Emission Spectrometer

For precise chemical composition analysis of molten metal before pouring, ensuring exact alloy grades.

Microscope

Metallurgical Microscope

With image analyzer software to check nodularity, nodule count, and matrix structure of Ductile Iron.

Hardness Tester

Brinell Hardness Tester

To measure the hardness of the final castings ensuring they meet the required mechanical properties.

UTM Machine

Universal Testing Machine

For measuring Tensile Strength, Yield Strength, and Elongation of test bars from each batch.

Sand Lab

Sand Testing Equipment

To check moisture, permeability, compression strength, and grain fineness of the moulding sand.

Dimensional Check

Dimensional Inspection

Using Vernier Calipers, Micrometers, Height Gauges, and gauges to verify all critical dimensions.

Standards

Certifications

ISO 9001:2015

Certified for Quality Management Systems in manufacturing and supply of castings.
