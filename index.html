Our Markets

Industries We Serve

Delivering high-integrity ductile and grey iron castings across critical global sectors.

Pumps & Valves

High-pressure pump bodies, split casings, impellers, and valve housings requiring tight grain structure.

Automotive & Gearbox

Heavy duty wheel hubs, brackets, gearbox housings, transmission parts, and structural castings.

Electric Motors
