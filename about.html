Our Story

Driven by Quality,
Defined by Precision.

Rudra Technocast is a premier manufacturer of high-quality Spheroidal Graphite Iron (Ductile Iron / SGI) and Grey Iron (CI) castings in Rajkot, Gujarat. From our inception, we have built a reputation for combining state-of-the-art foundry facilities with in-house CNC and VMC finishing capabilities under one roof.

Our financial and operational strengths allow us to continually invest in modern production machinery and advanced testing laboratories. The result is a highly efficient, integrated engineering facility capable of handling customized components from initial CAD design to ready-to-assemble finished components.

By conforming strictly to international grade standards (EN, ASTM, IS) and offering a flexible piece-weight manufacturing footprint, we provide global clients with reliable, long-term supply solutions they can trust.

Our Core Purpose

Vision, Mission & Values

Our Vision

To be globally recognized as a leading-edge, high-precision foundry, continuously raising standards in Ductile and Grey Iron casting through advanced metallurgy and processing technology.

Our Mission

To manufacture and deliver zero-defect castings with tailored machining configurations. We achieve this by deploying modern green sand moulding systems, in-house CNC/VMC machines, and strict quality assurance protocols.

Our Values

  • Uncompromising Metal Purity
  • Finished Parts Integration
  • Operational Transparency
  • Continuous Engineering Innovation
Capabilities

Our Core Competencies

Leveraging advanced technology and rigorous standards to deliver market-leading castings.

Standard Material Conformance

We comply fully with standard grades: Ductile Iron (SGI) EN-GJS-400-15 / 500-7, ASTM A536; and Grey Iron (CI) FG 200, FG 220, FG 260.

Advanced Casting Methods

Equipped for semi-automatic green sand moulding lines (box size up to 600×700×200mm), CO2 sand casting, and Shell Moulding.

Precision Machining Shop

Equipped with 3-axis VMC and CNC turning centers to machine complex profiles, drill, tap, and maintain tight dimensional tolerances.

Infrastructure

State-of-the-Art Plant

Our facility is equipped with the latest technology to ensure precision manufacturing at scale.
