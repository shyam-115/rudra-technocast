Manufacturing Journey

Step-by-Step Excellence

Our systematic approach guarantees precise, defect-free iron casting production at every stage.

01

Pattern & Mould Design

Our engineering team utilizes advanced CAD/CAM software to design accurate patterns based on customer requirements, ensuring perfect dimensional accuracy from the start.

CAD/CAM 3D Modeling

Sand Preparation & Moulding

We use high-quality silica sand mixed with binders in intensive sand mixers. The moulds are prepared using high-pressure moulding machines for superior surface finish.

Core Shooter Sand Plant
02
03

Melting & Alloying

Scrap iron and alloys are melted in state-of-the-art Induction Furnaces. Temperatures and chemical compositions are strictly monitored using spectrometers before pouring.

Induction Furnace Spectrometer

Pouring & Solidification

The treated molten metal is carefully poured into the prepared moulds. Proper cooling times are enforced to ensure the correct micro-structure and mechanical properties develop.

Ladle Pouring Controlled Cooling
04
05

Shakeout & Cleaning

Once solidified, the castings undergo a shakeout process to separate the sand. Shot blasting machines are then used to thoroughly clean the surface of the castings.

Vibratory Shakeout Shot Blasting

Machining & Finishing

Runners and risers are removed via grinding. If required by the client, CNC/VMC machining is performed to achieve exact tolerances and a ready-to-assemble finish.

CNC / VMC Fettling
06
07

Quality Inspection & Dispatch

Every batch undergoes rigorous lab testing (hardness, tensile strength, microstructure) before final packaging. Ready parts are dispatched securely to global destinations.

CMM Hardness Test Logistics
