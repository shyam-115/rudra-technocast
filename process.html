Manufacturing Journey

Step-by-Step Excellence

Our systematic approach guarantees precise, defect-free iron casting production at every stage.

01

Pattern & Mould Design

Our engineering team utilizes advanced CAD/CAM software to design accurate patterns based on customer requirements, ensuring perfect dimensional accuracy from the start.

CAD/CAM 3D Modeling

Sand Moulding & Preparation

Using our semi-automatic Green Sand moulding line, we prepare high-quality sand moulds. We support mould box sizes up to 600×700×200mm with a production speed of 25–30 moulds per hour for pieces ranging from 0.7 Kg to 100 Kg.

Green Sand Line Box: 600×700×200mm 25-30 moulds/hr
02
03

Melting & Alloying

Scrap iron and precise alloys are melted in our induction furnaces equipped with a 350 kW control panel. We operate dual crucibles (500 Kg and 300 Kg capacity) with spectrometer testing to verify correct chemistry before pouring.

350 kW Panel 500Kg & 300Kg Crucibles Spectrometer

Pouring & Solidification

Molten metal is transferred via crane and manual pouring using 500 Kg and 1000 Kg ladles. We utilize a modern T-spout ladle design to ensure clean, precise, and slag-free metal delivery into the moulds.

500Kg - 1000Kg Ladles T-Spout Ladle Crane Pouring
04
05

Shakeout & Fettling

Castings are separated from sand moulds and finished in our cleaning department. Fettling equipment includes 2× swing grinders, 25× flexible shaft grinders, and 10× hand grinders, followed by tumble blast shot-blasting for a clean surface finish.

Shot Blasting 25x Shaft Grinders Swing Grinders

Precision Machining

We provide in-house precision machining. Our machine shop is equipped with CNC turning centers and 3-axis VMC (Vertical Machining Centers) to handle complex profiles, pockets, drilling, tapping, and tight tolerances.

3-Axis VMC CNC Turning Drilling & Tapping
06
07

Quality Testing & Dispatch

Our quality assurance includes sand testing (permeability, moisture, strength), material testing (spectrometer chemistry check, hardness, tensile strength, and microstructure exam), and dimensional verification using CMM and profile gauges.

Spectrometer & CMM Sand Lab Hardness & Tensile
